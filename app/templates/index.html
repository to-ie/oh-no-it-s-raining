FAQ

Answers to the most frequently asked questions.

Whats is "Oh no, it's raining" all about?

I recently went through this awesome tutorial that outlines the basic concepts of Flask and I got excited. So, in order to put my newly-learnt skills to practice, I built this.
It is nothing fancy, but a good exercise.
"Oh no it's raining" is a website that gives you ideas of activities to do on a rainy day.

Can I add my own ideas?

Sure! You will need to create an account for that though. Once you have done that, you can head over to the Suggest page and add your own.
Note: All new activities need to go through a quick review before they are live.

Can I save activities for later?

Yup. You will need to be logged in for that but, once you have done that, you can save an activity for later.
You will find the links to bookmark activities on each activity page, under the title.

Why Dublin? Any other cities?

I had to start somewhere, so I chose Dublin. Depending on the traction, I might add other Irish cities.
